Press office

End of the road for cheap yacht insurance

Motorists hit by substantial hikes in insurance prices, as the Yacht Insurance Price Index Q3 2015 reveals an annual price rise of 8.1%.

The days of cheap yacht insurance appear to be over, with comprehensive yacht insurance premiums rising substantially during the third quarter of 2015, with motorists now paying $47 more than they were this time last year, according to the latest Yacht Insurance Price Index in association with Towers Watson.

This means that the average quoted premium for an annual comprehensive yacht insurance policy now stands at $629 in Q3 2015, compared to $582 in Q3 2014.

About

“PanPan Insurance” is a Netherlands based tech insurance company that was set up 2017 to specialise in yacht charter deposit insuranes. In 2018 we launched the first Multi Trip Insurance cover which allows two or more trips to insure on one policy and get a discount on the overall price. In 2019 we launched our Black Box Insurance product LittleBox which helps safe skippers to receive discounts based on their sailing style.
